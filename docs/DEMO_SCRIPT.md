# Four-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - Problem

MSME credit appraisal data is scattered across statements, GST-like filings, bureau-like checks, document gaps, transaction behavior, and relationship-manager notes. LendSignal 360 turns those fragments into a banker-facing credit file for human review.

## 0:30 - Case Inbox

Open `/case-inbox`. Show lanes for ready review, missing evidence, risk attention, high potential prospects, and low confidence cases. Pick a case that needs action.

## 1:00 - Credit File

Open the selected case. Show identity, financial records, documents, derived signals, credit posture, Copilot, and audit sections. Point out score, risk tier, confidence, current blocker, and next human action.

## 1:35 - Data Room

Open `/data-room`. Show organized records and missing evidence. Explain that unavailable actions are disabled because the MVP does not pretend to have unsupported workflow services.

## 2:05 - Evidence Map

Open `/evidence-map`. Show the chain from source data to derived signal, score component, lending question, and human action. Emphasize traceability and missing-data honesty.

## 2:40 - Credit Copilot

Open `/copilot` or the Copilot panel in the Credit File. Ask: "Why is this case blocked?" Show that the answer cites internal inputs, includes assumptions, and keeps the deterministic score unchanged.

## 3:20 - Human Action And Audit

Show recommended officer action and audit trail. Explain that the system guides next review steps but does not make final credit decisions.

## 3:50 - Close

Close with the roadmap: bank-safe decision support today, future adapters for Account Aggregator, ULI, GST, Udyam, GeM, document parsing, PostgreSQL persistence, auth, and observability.
